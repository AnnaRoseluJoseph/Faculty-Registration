IIITM CENTRAL LIBRARY

Welcome to the Central Libray which is one of the central support services of IIT Madras. The mission of the Central Library is to provide information services and access to bibliographic and full text digital and printed resources to support the scholarly and informational needs of the Institute Community. The Central Library is well equipped with modern facilities and resources in the form of CD-ROMs, On-line databases, audio video cassettes, books, e-journals, patents, e-standards, theses, reports, monographs etc. The library homepage will provide electronic access to various full text & bibliographical databases & e-journals. The Central Library has 9001:2008 certification by TUV NORD of Germany along with other units of the Institute for the establishment and maintenance of quality library system, services & procedures.
